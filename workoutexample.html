Workout!!

Bench Press

The bench press is a fundamental upper body exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Deadlift

The deadlift is a compound exercise that works the entire posterior chain, including the back, glutes, and hamstrings.

Pull-up

The pull-up is a bodyweight exercise that primarily targets the back and arms, helping to build upper body strength.

Shoulder Press

The shoulder press is an upper body exercise that targets the deltoids and triceps, helping to build shoulder strength.

Squat

The squat is a fundamental lower body exercise that targets the quadriceps, glutes, and hamstrings.
